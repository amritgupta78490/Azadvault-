About Azad Vault

Welcome to Azad Vault. This platform is designed to provide simple and useful tools like a Biodata Maker and high-quality wallpapers for users.

My name is Amrit Kumar Gupta. I am a 15-year-old student and beginner developer. Along with my studies, I created this website to learn and build something useful for people.

Azad Vault focuses on simplicity, speed, and ease of use. All tools are designed to work directly on your device without storing your personal data.

I am continuously working to improve this platform and add better features in the future.
